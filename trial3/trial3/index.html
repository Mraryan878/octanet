    Your problem is our concern.

    Our platform serves as a central hub where citizens, government officials, and community organizations can come together to collaborate, share ideas, and take action on wide range of critical issues affecting society. From water suply and infrastructure to electiricity, waste management, hospitality, and corruption, EngageCitizen empowers individuals to play an active role in finding solutions for these complex challenges.



    Problems that we look for





    Infrastructure

    Infrastructure development is a crucial aspect for sustaining economic growth and improving the quality of life for its citizens. Despite significant progress in recent years, challenges such as inadequate transportation networks, insufficient power generation capacity, and deficient urban infrastructure persist.



    Water Management

    Water management in India faces multifaceted challenges stemming from factors such as population growth, urbanization, industrialization, and climate change. Issues include water scarcity in some regions, pollution of water bodies due to untreated sewage and industrial waste, and inefficient distribution and management systems.



    Health Care

    Healthcare in India is characterized by a complex landscape, with a mix of public and private providers serving a diverse population across urban and rural areas. Challenges include inadequate infrastructure, uneven access to healthcare services, and disparities in quality and affordability.






    Electricity

    Electricity in India is a vital component of the country's infrastructure, driving economic growth and enhancing quality of life. Despite significant progress in increasing electricity access in recent years, challenges such as inadequate generation capacity, transmission losses, and distribution inefficiencies persist.
